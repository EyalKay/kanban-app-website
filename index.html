A fast, keyboard-driven desktop Kanban board with built-in notes.

Download The Taskinator Windows · Free · Lightweight

Features

  • Kanban Board 4 columns — Backlog, To-Do, Doing, Done. Move tasks with quick buttons or keyboard shortcuts.
  • Braindump Notes Built-in markdown editor with syntax highlighting and auto-save. Turn selected text into tasks instantly.
  • Dashboard & Reminders Statistics overview, overdue/due-today counts, and a reminders screen organized by urgency.
  • Quick Capture Floating popup window to jot down thoughts without leaving your current workflow.
  • Keyboard-First Global shortcuts for search, task creation, note navigation. Designed for speed.
  • Priorities & Due Dates P1/P2/P3 priority levels with color coding. Due date tracking with overdue indicators.
  • Dark & Light Themes Clean dark theme by default. Switch to light mode in settings.
  • Lightweight & Native Built with Tauri — small binary, low memory, no Electron bloat. Runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
